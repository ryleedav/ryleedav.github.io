Gracie Abrams' Discography

Background

Synopsis

Abrams' music is a blend of pop, synth-pop, folk, indie-pop.

The 25-year-old released her debut single "Mean It" in 2019, and debut EP Minor.
She released a second EP This Is What It Feels Like in 2021. Abrams debut album Good Riddance was released in February, and the deluxe in June.
She has performed as an opener for Olivia Rodrigo for the Sour Tour and Taylor Swift in the Era's Tour.
Abrams second album The Secret of Us was released in June and the deluxe in November where "That's So True" reached number four on the Billboard charts.

Accolades

  • Billboard Women in Music - Songwriter of the Year
  • iHeartRadio Music Awards - Social Star of the Year
  • Grammy Awards - Nominated for Best New Artist and Best Pop Duo/Group Performance
