## About this Accessibility / Flare Project


### Team description; describe team content area; use bullets to list team names

Our team focused on what cognitive load is, how to reduce it, and how it relates to accessibility; studying types of disabilities, and types of assistive technologies to help. We investigated how chunking and skimmability of text, as well as features such as transcripts are helpful for effective accessibility. We looked into various best practices and examples of accessible content.
